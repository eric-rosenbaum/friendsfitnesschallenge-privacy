FriendsFitnessChallenge Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FriendsFitnessChallenge (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the FriendsFitnessChallenge mobile application (the “App”).

Last updated: February 24, 2026
Quick summary: We use your info to run the app (login, groups, workout logging, progress, leaderboards). We don’t sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Display name

User Content

Information you provide while using the App, including:

  • Workout logs (cardio and strength activity)
  • Group membership and participation data
  • Challenge progress and completion status

Technical Information

We may collect limited technical data for performance and debugging, such as:

  • Anonymous usage data
  • Device type and operating system

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the App’s features
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Track progress and display leaderboards
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Respond to support requests

3. How Your Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal data.

Information may be shared only:

  • With other members of your group (e.g., progress, logs, leaderboards)
  • With service providers (such as Supabase) that securely host our backend
  • If required by law

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using trusted cloud infrastructure. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Update your profile information within the App
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at ericrosenbaum7@gmail.com

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services for authentication and data storage (such as Supabase). These providers process data on our behalf in accordance with their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated “Last updated” date.
